Site Plan for the Mildred Online Store

Site Name: SERENA MILDRED ONLINE BOUTIQUE (SMOB)

The site was named after my youngest child Serena Mildred, who loves to pretend she has wares to sell. She turned 4 on June 13th, 2024. While preparing for her birthday the idea of building an online shop got stuck in my mind.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to give me a platform to reach out to visitors and customers to make purchases of their sports wears, clothing, apparels and accessories online while making the process seamless and intuitive. Products will be displayed along with some information such as the price, current status, customer reviews, along with a form for a newsletter subscription.
